Overview

We don't just manage paperwork — we protect the integrity of the work behind it. That same standard governs how we handle your personal information.

Topaz Admin Solutions ("Topaz," "we," "us," or "our") provides professional administrative and paralegal support services to legal professionals. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our website (topazadminsolutions.com), contact us, or engage our services.

We are committed to handling all personal information — yours and that of your clients — with the same standard of precision and confidentiality we bring to every engagement. Please read this policy carefully. By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described herein.

If you are a current or prospective client, a separate service agreement and confidentiality agreement will govern the information shared in the course of our professional relationship. This policy applies to website visitors and general communications only.

2

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

We collect personal information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Complete and submit our contact form (name, email address, phone number, and message content)
  • Send us an email or call our office
  • Request a consultation or engage our services
  • Subscribe to any communications we may offer

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, certain technical information is collected automatically through standard web server logs and analytics tools:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location (city/region level)
  • Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on each page, and referring URL
  • Date and time of your visit
  • Device type (desktop, tablet, or mobile)

Information we do not collect

We do not collect payment card numbers, Social Security numbers, government-issued identification, or sensitive personal data through this website. If such information is required in the course of providing services, it is handled solely through secure, encrypted channels governed by a separate agreement.

3

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for legitimate business purposes. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries — to answer questions submitted through our contact form or by email and phone
  • Provide our services — to perform administrative, paralegal, or related support services you have engaged us to deliver
  • Improve our website — to understand how visitors use our site and to enhance performance and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations — to meet applicable laws, regulations, court orders, or professional responsibility requirements
  • Maintain security — to detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information for marketing purposes. We do not use your information to build advertising profiles, and we do not engage in behavioral advertising.

4

Sharing & Disclosure

We treat your personal information with strict confidentiality. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

Service providers

We may share information with trusted vendors who assist us in operating this website and conducting our business (for example, web hosting providers, email delivery services, and analytics platforms). These providers are contractually restricted from using your information for any purpose other than providing services to us, and they are required to maintain appropriate security standards.

Legal and professional obligations

We may disclose information when required by law, subpoena, court order, or legal process; to comply with applicable professional responsibility rules; to protect the rights, property, or safety of Topaz, our clients, or others; or to enforce our agreements.

Business transitions

In the unlikely event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or substantially all of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or prominent notice on this website of any such change in ownership or control.

With your consent

We may share your information in other ways with your explicit consent at the time of sharing.

5

Data Security

Security is not an afterthought at Topaz — it is a prerequisite for earning and maintaining your trust. We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • TLS/SSL encryption for all data transmitted to and from this website
  • Access controls limiting personal data to personnel with a legitimate need
  • Regular review of our data collection, storage, and processing practices
  • Secure, encrypted channels for sharing sensitive client materials

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. If you have reason to believe that your interaction with us is no longer secure, please contact us immediately.

6

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, to maintain our business records, or to meet legal, ethical, or professional obligations. Our general retention guidelines are:

  • Website inquiry data (contact form submissions) — retained for up to 24 months from initial contact, unless a service engagement follows, in which case longer retention may apply under our service agreement
  • Client service records — retained in accordance with applicable legal and professional responsibility requirements, typically a minimum of five (5) years following the conclusion of an engagement
  • Website analytics data — retained in aggregated, anonymized form for up to 26 months

When personal information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it in accordance with industry best practices.

7

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights with respect to your personal information. Regardless of jurisdiction, we honor the following requests from all individuals:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal information, subject to our legal and professional retention obligations
  • Portability — request that we provide your personal information in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to the processing of your personal information for a particular purpose
  • Withdrawal of consent — where processing is based on your consent, withdraw that consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 12. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request, and we reserve the right to decline requests that are legally prohibited or that would require a disproportionate effort.

8

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses a minimal, privacy-respecting approach to cookies and similar tracking technologies.

Essential cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be disabled. They do not store any personally identifiable information and are not used for tracking or advertising.

Analytics

We may use privacy-focused analytics tools configured to anonymize IP addresses and disable cross-site tracking to understand aggregate usage of our website. These tools do not build individual user profiles. Aggregated, anonymized analytics data helps us improve website content and user experience.

Your choices

Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies through browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect some website functionality. Our website does not change its behavior in response to Do Not Track browser signals because we do not engage in the kind of tracking those signals are designed to address.

We do not use cookies for advertising, retargeting, or cross-site behavioral tracking of any kind.

9

Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not operated by Topaz Admin Solutions. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

Our website loads web fonts from Google Fonts and Fontshare. These requests may transmit your IP address to their servers. Both services have their own privacy practices; we recommend reviewing their policies if this is a concern. We do not use social media plug-ins, embedded tracking pixels, or third-party advertising networks.

10

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are directed exclusively to legal professionals and business clients. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or under the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately and we will take prompt steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, applicable law, or operational needs. When we make material changes, we will update the "Effective" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, provide additional notice (such as a notice on our homepage or an email to known contacts).

Your continued use of this website following the posting of changes constitutes your acceptance of those changes. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
